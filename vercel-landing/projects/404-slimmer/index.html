Features

Everything you need to tame 404 errors

Smart 404 Logging

Automatically captures every 404 hit with URL, referrer, user agent, timestamp, and hit count. Know exactly which broken links are draining your resources.

One-Click Redirects

Set up 301/302 redirects directly from the 404 log. Fix broken links instantly without touching .htaccess or installing another plugin.

Auto Cleanup

Schedule automatic cleanup of old 404 logs to keep your database lean. Set retention period (7, 30, 90 days) and let the plugin handle the rest.

Email Notifications

Get notified when a URL hits a configurable 404 threshold. Catch broken links early before they impact your SEO or user experience.

Advanced Filtering

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Does 404 Slimmer slow down my site? +

Not at all. 404 Slimmer only hooks into WordPress when a 404 error actually occurs. On normal page loads, it adds zero overhead. The logging uses a single lightweight database insert.

Is it compatible with caching plugins? +

Yes. 404 Slimmer works alongside all major caching plugins (WP Rocket, LiteSpeed Cache, W3 Total Cache, etc.). Since 404 pages are typically not cached, the plugin captures them reliably.

Can I migrate redirects from another plugin? +

The Agency plan includes bulk import/export in CSV format, making it easy to migrate from Redirection, Yoast, or Rank Math redirects. Pro users can set up redirects manually from the dashboard.

What happens when my license expires? +

The plugin continues to work. You just won't receive updates or priority support until you renew. Your 404 logs and redirects remain intact.
